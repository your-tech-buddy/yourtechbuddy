About Us

We are a team from Barcelona with expertise in data analytics. Our mission is to help small and medium-sized businesses automate their data-related processes, enabling them to fully leverage their data and focus on what they do best: their core business operations.

Our Approach

At Your Tech Buddy, we understand the challenges that small and medium-sized businesses face when it comes to handling large amounts of data. By automating repetitive tasks and streamlining processes, we free up your time and resources so you can make better, data-driven decisions without getting bogged down in technical details.

Our goal is to provide tailored solutions that help you automate processes related to data collection, analysis, reporting, and more. This allows you to focus on growing your business while we ensure your data is working for you, providing insights and driving efficiency.
